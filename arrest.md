# If You're Being Arrested (India)

**Disclaimer:** General information only. Not legal advice. Verify with a lawyer; state-specific rules may apply.

## Immediate Steps
1. Stay calm. Do **not** resist or flee.
2. Ask: **"Am I under arrest?"** If yes, request the **grounds of arrest**.
3. Provide basic identity details only. You have the **right to remain silent** beyond identity details.
4. Ask to contact a **lawyer** and a **friend/family**. Police must allow this within reasonable time.
5. Note the **police station name, officer names/badge**, date & time.

## Your Rights (General)
- You must be informed of the **grounds of arrest**.
- You have a right to **legal counsel**.
- A **memo of arrest** should be prepared with a witness signature (relative or respected person).
- A **medical examination** should be conducted if you allege injury.
- Women should not be arrested after sunset and before sunrise except in exceptional circumstances.
- Produce before a magistrate within **24 hours** (excluding travel time).

## What to Say
- "I wish to remain silent until my lawyer is present."
- "Please tell me the grounds for my arrest."
- "I request to call my emergency contact and lawyer."

## What to Avoid
- Do not argue or obstruct. Avoid signing blank or unclear documents.
- Do not offer bribes or make threats.

## Helpful
- Record details discreetly (time, place, witnesses). If safe, note **vehicle number** and **officer details**.

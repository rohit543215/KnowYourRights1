# Harassment / Threats (India)

**Disclaimer:** General information only. Not legal advice.

## Steps
1. If in immediate danger, call **112**.
2. Preserve evidence: screenshots, messages, emails, call logs.
3. For workplace harassment, escalate per **POSH** policy; contact the Internal Committee.
4. For cyber harassment, file a complaint at the **National Cyber Crime Portal** and local police.
5. Seek support from trusted contacts or NGOs; consider counseling.

## What to Say
- "I want to file a complaint regarding harassment."

## What to Avoid
- Deleting evidence.

# Road Accident (India)

**Disclaimer:** General information only. Not legal advice.

## Immediate Steps
1. Ensure safety: move to a safe spot if possible; switch on hazard lights.
2. Check for injuries and call **112** (emergency) or **108** (ambulance). Provide precise location.
3. Do **not** run away. Good Samaritans helping victims have legal protections.
4. Take photos/videos of the scene, vehicles, license plates, and injuries (if any).
5. Exchange names, numbers, and insurance details. Collect witness contacts.

## Reporting
- Inform the nearest **police station**. File an **FIR** if required.
- For minor damage, consider a **Motor Vehicle Accident Report** and insurance claim process.

## Insurance
- Notify your insurer within the stipulated time. Keep bills, reports, and photos.

## What to Avoid
- Do not admit fault at the scene.
- Do not accept quick cash settlements without documentation.
